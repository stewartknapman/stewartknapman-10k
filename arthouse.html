Commercial Website for Arthouse Architects

Role:
Design & Development Lead as part of Lucid.
Date:
Mid 2015.
Platform:
Siteleaf

Having won a host of awards and being featured extensively in the countries top architectural & design magazines, Arthouse Architects are one of Nelsons premier architectural agencies.

Lucid first built the Arthouse website in 2010, and in 2015 as they were looking to open a second office in Christchurch, it was decided that a refresh was in order.

We wanted to showcase both the stories as well as the impressive photography of their work, so we leant heavily on a magazine-like style. With bold typography and large, immersive imagery, we were able to create a series of small articles that would be at home in both web and print.
