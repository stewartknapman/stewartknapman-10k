Six‑Pack Builder for PHD

Role:
Development Lead as part of Lucid.
Date:
Mid to late 2015.
Platform:
Shopify

Pure Health Delivered (PHD) create and deliver cold-pressed juices, cleanses, and meals for busy, health conscious people.

In mid 2015 they approached us with the task of moving their then current site from Magento to Shopify. One of the key requirements was to create an interface for their customers to enable them to build a customised six-pack of juices. Because of time and budget constraints we weren't able to build a full blown app to handle the bundled products so we opted to build it directly into the Shopify theme.

The resulting interface has been hugely successful for them and lead to other bundle-type interfaces within the site, as well as lot of enquires by other Shopify merchants for similar work.
